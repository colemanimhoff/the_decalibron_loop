The Decalibron Loop

By Coleman Imhoff

Summit Four 14ers in a Single Day

Are you looking into hike your first 14,000 ft. mountain? Or are you one of those die-hard hikers trying to knock off every 14er in Colorado? The “Decalibron Loop” offers a unique opportunity to summit four 14ers in a single day! Located in the Mosquito Range in Colorado, this loop includes:

  • Mt. Democrat (14,148')
  • Mt. Cameron (14,238')
  • Mt. Bross (14,172')
  • Mt. Lincoln (14,286')
Looking Back At Mt. Democrat

Start your journey at Kite Lake Trail Head at 12,000 ft. Plan on camping the night before and waking up at dusk the following morning to begin hiking. Despite only being 7.5 miles long with an elevation gain of 3,000 total ft, this hike is difficult and will take a full day. Make sure you plan accordingly and come prepared!

On the Summit of Mt. Cameron

What Should I Bring?

  1. Water
  2. Warm Layers
  3. First Aid Kit
  4. Snacks
  5. Sunscreen and Sunglasses
  6. Beer (having a beer at the summit is a fun tradition!)

Do Your Research!

Make a plan. Weather can be unpredictable and hiking at high altitudes can be dangerous. Here is a list of some helpful resources:
